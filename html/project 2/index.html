How to make the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of flour
  • 5 eggs
  • Cinnamon
  • 2 tsps of salt
  • 5 tsps of sugar
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast
    • Pour warm milk into a bowl
    • Add yeast + 1 tbsp sugar
    • Let it sit 5–10 minutes
  2. Make the dough
    • Add melted butter, egg, remaining sugar, and salt
    • Mix well
    • Slowly add flour and mix until dough forms
  3. Knead the dough
    • Put dough on a floured surface
    • Knead for 8–10 minutes until soft and elastic
    • Shape into a ball
  4. Let it rise
    • Place dough in a greased bowl
    • Cover with a towel
    • Let rise for 1 hour (until doubled)
  5. Make the cinnamon filling
    • Brown sugar
    • Cinnamon
  6. Roll out the dough
    • Roll dough into a rectangle
    • Spread softened butter all over
    • Sprinkle cinnamon sugar evenly
  7. Roll & cut
    • Roll tightly from the long side
    • Cut into 8–12 rolls (use a knife or dental floss)
  8. Second rise
    • Place rolls in a greased baking dish
    • Cover and rest for 30 minutes
  9. Bake
    • Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F)
    • Bake for 20–25 minutes
    • Tops should be golden brown
  10. Make glaze (optional)
    • Mix powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla
    • Pour over warm rolls
